Schedule A

  • Daily Featured "Deals" will be located at City-Slicking.com
  • A "Campaign" may consist of one or multiple "Deals".
  • Featured Deals start at midnight.
  • Featured Deals will typically run for 24 hours.
  • Discount code delivery is billed in one of four ways as indicated:
    • SMS delivered discount codes -- $0.50 each
    • Email and browser based reservation messages -- $0.65 each
    • Email and browser based discount codes which are only delivered upon request after the reservation messages -- $1.10 each
    • Codes delivered to GPS capable smartphones with location services running -- $2 each
  • When discount codes are delivered, they also have an expiration time/date which is viewable with the code. The Merchant can choose at his/her discretion to honor the expiration time/date.
  • All unexpired discount codes are to be honored throughout the whole length of the campaign.
  • Printed discount codes are never to be honored.
  • Once a smartphone Deal is opted for and used, the registered user will not be sent the same Deal a second time on the phone. However, return discounts will be offered as well as incentives to share the deal with friends if they are included in the campaign.
  • On both the website and the mobile site there will be a link to key information about the Deal which may include:
  • an explanation of the Deal
  • the address and phone number of the merchant
  • a map of the merchants location
  • an article about the merchant
  • phone number of the merchant
  • other information as necessary
  • The terms of use will be displayed on the Deal.
    • Example only: Restaurant
      • 50% off
      • Limit one per table
      • Maximum discount $50 off bill
      • Redeemable at: (locations)

Reporting to Merchant by CitySlicking, LLC

  • An invoice representing the summary accounting for the Deal’s discount codes sent during the featured deal period will be sent the day after the close of the 24 hour Featured Deal. Subsequently billings will follow monthly until the close of the campaign. Payment on all invoices is due NET 15 days from invoice date
  • Each time a Deal is delivered using location services in/near the store, we will track the user, date, time, location.
  • We will report online the deal’s discount code delivery stats and they will be stated on the monthly invoice.

Provided at No Charge

  • We will work with you to develop a high perceived consumer value for you campaign and the design for your Deal(s). Will also consult with regards to achieving customer loyalty and appropriate follow up communications we can facilitate.
  • We will work with you on writing a descriptive Article about your business which may contain photos.
  • We will notify all City-Slicking users in your area and post your Deal(s) on City-Slicking websites
  • We will donate approximately 10% of our net profit from your campaign to a local charity.
