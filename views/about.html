About Us

City-Slicking is all about finding and sharing the best discount deals in your area. In addition, we think the stand out content from our troop of bloggers will provide you the inside scoop on food, dining, theatre, film, events, the community and myriad off the wall fun things. And each time you use your City-Slicking deal, you’re helping us and your local merchant to give back to charities in your local area. What’s not to like???

We’re not your traditional VC sponsored corporate moguls but rather an eclectic group of eager beavers. We’re having fun and feeling adventurous.
